Description
-----------

.. only:: (not man)

   .. binary:: mongosqld

.. include:: /includes/fact-bi-enterprise.rst

:program:`mongosqld` accepts incoming requests from a MySQL client, and
proxies those requests to a :program:`mongod` or :program:`mongos`
instance.
